Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, participate in quizzes, or communicate with us. This may include your username, points earned, and quiz preferences.

We use the information we collect to maintain and improve our services, track quiz progress, display local leaderboards, and ensure a fair reward system for all players.

Your privacy is our priority. We implement high-level security measures to protect your progress and personal data. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
